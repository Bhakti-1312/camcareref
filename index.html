Mental Health Status of College Students (Jammu & Kashmir, 2024)

  • 12.5% students reported severe depression (PHQ-9)
  • 24.3% students reported severe anxiety (GAD-7)
  • 19.2% students reported high perceived stress
  • Females showed higher prevalence & risk (OR ≈ 1.6–1.7)
  • Indicates strong need for campus mental health support systems

More than half of students experience significant mental health challenges, with anxiety and stress being the most common.
